Frequently Asked Questions

What is the functionality of SMS/Email notifications in a cannabis dispensary WordPress plugin?
The functionality includes automatically sending out text message (SMS) or email notifications to customers when their cannabis order is ready for pickup or delivery. This feature improves customer service by providing timely updates and helps streamline dispensary operations.
How can I set up SMS/Email notifications for order readiness in my cannabis dispensary WordPress plugin?
To set up these notifications, youll need to configure the settings within your WordPress plugin. This usually involves integrating with an SMS gateway or email service provider, setting up message templates, and specifying the conditions under which messages should be sent (e.g., when an order changes to a ready status).
Are there any privacy concerns with sending SMS/Email notifications for cannabis orders?
Yes, since cannabis-related communication can be sensitive, its important to ensure that all messages are compliant with privacy laws and regulations such as HIPAA in the U.S. Customers should opt-in to receive these notifications, and care should be taken not to reveal specific details about their order in the messages.
Can I customize the content of the SMS/Email notifications sent from my cannabis dispensary WordPress plugin?
Typically, yes. Most plugins will allow you to create custom templates for your messages where you can specify what information is included in the notification. You should provide clear and concise information while also ensuring compliance with any legal restrictions on communication about cannabis products.
